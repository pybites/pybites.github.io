4 things that stop you from getting there

There are 4 common hurdles that prevent people from getting to this level:

  • They spend way too much time on books, videos and courses (aka tutorial paralysis), no time actually shipping software.
  • They are comfortable in the realm of scripting (and consider themselves scripters), but shy away from taking on bigger projects (spoiler alert: This is exactly what gets you from an intermediate to advanced Pythonista!)
  • They think they lack the required developer skills to pull it off (We commonly hear: How do I best structure my app? How do I write quality / maintainable code? How should I test my code?)
  • Hampered by Imposter Syndrome, they are not putting their code / app / project out there and are missing out on crucial feedback loops (e.g. code critique) that are necessary to grow. Remember, you don't know what you don't know ...

And don't think it's just you, we've seen many people being crippled by one, or more often, all of these roadblocks.
