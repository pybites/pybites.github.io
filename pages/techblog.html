The One Thing Every Programmer Should Do ...

Do you have a Python blog?

If you landed on this page the answer is probably No. But don't worry, we are going to fix this together ...

When you are a programmer one of the most important seeds that you can plant and cultivate is a tech blog.

It not only will shape your tech and communication skills, it will actually become your gravitational asset that will continuously attract opportunity.

That is, people will reach out to you instead of the other way around.

In the training below we will show you how to get a tech blog up and running in ~20 min using Github Pages and Pelican.

We will also cover what to write about and how PyBites started as just a blog and how we leveraged this vehicle to niche down and seek new opportunity.

Get ready to take some serious action this month ... and remember: keep it fun as you go!

- Bob and Julian (PyBites)
